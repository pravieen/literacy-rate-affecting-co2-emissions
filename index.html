LITERACY EFFECTING CO2 Emission Rate

The level of literacy in a society can indeed have an impact on CO2 emission rates. Higher literacy rates are often associated with increased awareness and knowledge about environmental issues, including the impact of CO2 emissions on climate change. When individuals are literate, they are more likely to understand the importance of sustainable practices and make informed choices that reduce their carbon footprint.
